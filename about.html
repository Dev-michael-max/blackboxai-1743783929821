About NeoMeet

We're reimagining virtual collaboration for the modern workplace

Our Story

Founded in 2020, NeoMeet was born out of frustration with existing video conferencing tools that were clunky, unreliable, and lacked the features needed for truly productive collaboration.

Our team of remote work veterans set out to build a platform that would not only make meetings better, but transform how teams work together across distances.

Today, NeoMeet powers thousands of organizations worldwide with our intuitive, feature-rich platform that brings people together in meaningful ways.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To empower teams to do their best work together, no matter where they are located, through intuitive and powerful collaboration tools.

Innovation

We constantly push boundaries to develop features that anticipate and solve real collaboration challenges before they arise.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We listen, adapt, and evolve based on their needs and feedback.

Our Journey

Company Founded

March 2020

NeoMeet was founded by a team of remote work enthusiasts who saw the need for better virtual collaboration tools.

First Product Launch

September 2020

Released our MVP with basic video conferencing features to a small group of beta testers.

Public Launch
